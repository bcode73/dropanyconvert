How to convert PNG to JPG

  1. Upload your PNG file by dragging and dropping, pasting (Ctrl+V), or clicking to browse.
  2. Adjust quality using the slider. 85 is a good default — higher values keep more detail but produce larger files.
  3. Click "Convert" — conversion happens instantly in your browser. Nothing is uploaded.
  4. Download your JPG. Multiple files are packaged as a single ZIP automatically.
